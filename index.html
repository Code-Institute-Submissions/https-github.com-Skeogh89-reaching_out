About

Face coverings catch droplets. Droplets carry viruses and bacteria, including Covid 19. It's a simple, free thing we can all do for the short periods we're in shops, on public transport or otherwise close to lots of other people. It'll help slow the spread of the virus and allow us to get back to as close to normal as possible as quickly as possible.

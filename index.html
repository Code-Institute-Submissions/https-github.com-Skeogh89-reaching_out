Face coverings catch droplets. Droplets carry viruses and bacteria, including Covid 19. It's a simple, free thing we can all do for the short periods we're in shops, on public transport or otherwise close to lots of other people. The goal of this website is to provide masks for the local comunuty as it has become mandoroy to wear one in social situations. People are still cocooning to protect themselves from COVID-19 nad we are here to support them. If you require a Mask or support while cocooning please do not hesitete to contact us.

Re-usable Cloth Mask

Cloth face masks can be used for source control to reduce disease transmission arising from the wearer's respiratory droplets.During the COVID-19 pandemic, multiple countries recommended the use of cloth masks to reduce the spread of the virus.

Cocooning

Cocooning generally involves staying at home and reducing face-to-face interaction with other people to the greatest extent possible. It is intended to minimise your risk of contracting COVID-19.

Family, friends and neighbours can support you to get food and medical supplies. They must follow physical distancing guidelines. Where possible, you should use online shopping services.

However if you have none of these options please contact us for assistance
